📦 Product Information

WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) wall panels are made from a blend of materials:

  • 35% HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) - Provides durability and water resistance
  • 60% Wood Fiber (Wood Flour) - Gives natural wood appearance and texture
  • 5% Additives - Including colorants, coupling agents, UV stabilizers, and lubricants

This unique composition combines the natural aesthetics of wood with the durability and moisture resistance of plastic. Unlike real wood, WPC panels will not rot, warp, or splinter, making them ideal for long-term installations.

WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) panels contain real wood fiber, giving them:

  • More natural wood-like appearance and texture
  • Better thermal insulation properties
  • More substantial, heavier feel
  • Better sound absorption

PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) panels are made of plastic only:

  • Lightweight and easy to install
  • More affordable price point
  • 100% waterproof
  • Available in more surface finishes (glossy, matte, patterns)
  • Better for ceiling applications due to lightweight

Both are excellent choices for interior decoration. WPC is preferred for feature walls and premium applications, while PVC is ideal for broad ceiling and wall coverage.

Yes! All our WPC and PVC wall panels meet B1 fire resistance rating according to EN 14041:2004+A1:2008 European standards.

B1 classification means:

  • Difficult to ignite - requires significant heat to catch fire
  • Self-extinguishing - stops burning when ignition source is removed
  • Low smoke emission
  • No dripping of burning material

This makes our products suitable for commercial buildings, public spaces, and residential applications where fire safety is a priority.

Yes, 100% waterproof.

Our WPC panels have a water absorption rate of ≤0.5%, compared to wood which can absorb 20-30% of its weight in water. This means:

  • No swelling, warping, or rotting in humid conditions
  • Ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, basements
  • Suitable for outdoor sheltered areas
  • Mold and mildew resistant
  • Easy to clean with water

Unlike wood or wood-based panels (like MDF or plywood), our panels maintain their structural integrity and appearance even when exposed to water.

No formaldehyde. Our products are certified E0 level.

Formaldehyde emission: <0.1mg/L

This is significantly below international safety standards:

  • E0 (Best): ≤0.5mg/L
  • E1 (Standard): ≤1.5mg/L
  • E2 (Limited): ≤5.0mg/L

Our products are safe for indoor use, including:

  • Homes with children
  • Bedrooms and nurseries
  • Healthcare facilities
  • Schools and kindergartens

We can provide SGS test reports confirming formaldehyde levels upon request.

Our WPC panels are designed to last 25+ years with proper installation and use.

Key durability features:

  • UV stabilizers prevent color fading
  • Anti-aging compounds resist degradation
  • Weatherproof construction for both indoor and outdoor use
  • Scratch and impact resistant surface
  • No maintenance required (unlike wood)

For outdoor applications, we recommend our enhanced UV-resistant formulations which are specifically designed for prolonged sun exposure.
