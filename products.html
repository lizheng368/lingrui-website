All our wall panels meet B1 fire resistance standards, the highest rating for building materials. They are self-extinguishing and prevent flame spread.

💧

100% Waterproof

With water absorption rate ≤0.5%, our panels are perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, and other humid environments. No swelling, warping, or rotting.

Formaldehyde emission <0.1mg/L, meeting E0 environmental standards. Safe for indoor use, ideal for homes with children and pets.
