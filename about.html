Our Story

Zhejiang Yunshangguang New Materials Co., Ltd. (YUNSHANGGUANG) was established in 2016, but our journey in the building materials industry began in 2001. Over the past 25 years, we have evolved from a small workshop into a modern enterprise specializing in innovative eco-friendly building materials.

Our product portfolio includes WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) wall panels, PVC ceiling panels, carbon crystal boards, UV marble sheets, SPC flooring, and decorative profiles. These products are widely used in residential renovations, commercial spaces, hotels, and office buildings worldwide.

As a factory-direct manufacturer, we combine competitive pricing with uncompromising quality. Our state-of-the-art production facility spans 20,000 square meters, equipped with 20+ advanced production lines capable of producing 30,000 square meters daily.
